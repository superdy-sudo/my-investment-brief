## Quarter: Q1 FY2026 (reported October 29, 2025)

## Beat / Miss
- BEAT on both EPS and Revenue
- EPS: $4.13 vs estimate (beat by 13.15%)
- Revenue: $77.6 billion vs estimate (beat by 3.62%)
- YoY EPS growth: +25.2%
- YoY Revenue growth: +18.4%

## Key Drivers
- Azure cloud growth: +40% YoY
- Azure AI services growth accelerating
- Microsoft 365 Copilot: 100+ million monthly active users

## Guidance (Q2 FY2026)
- Revenue guidance: $79.5B - $80.6B (midpoint $80.05B)
- Analyst consensus: $79.95B - guidance was in line
- Capex will increase sequentially (concern point)

## Management Tone
- Positive on cloud and AI demand
- CFO Amy Hood flagged capex growth rate for FY2026 will exceed FY2025 rate
- Market reacted negatively to capex news: stock -3.7% after-hours

## Key Concern
- Capex for Q1 FY2026: $34.9 billion - significantly elevated
- Question: when does AI infrastructure spending translate to proportional revenue?
