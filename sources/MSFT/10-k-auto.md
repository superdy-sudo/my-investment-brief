# Microsoft (MSFT) - 10-K Annual Report Key Highlights
Source: SEC EDGAR Filing FY2025 (July 30, 2025, Accession: 0000950170-25-100235)
URL: https://last10k.com/sec-filings/msft/0000950170-25-100235.htm

## Revenue Trend
- FY2025 Revenue: $281.7 billion (+15% YoY)
- Azure surpassed $75 billion annual revenue for the first time (+34% YoY)
- Q1 FY2026 Revenue: $77.6 billion (+18.4% YoY) - beat estimate by 3.62%
- Azure AI services growth: +40% YoY in Q1 FY2026

## Margins
- Operating margins remain stable (reported as "stable" in analyst coverage)
- High-margin cloud and software mix continues to improve overall profitability
- Microsoft 365 Copilot surpassed 100 million monthly active users

## Moat / Competitive Advantages
- High switching costs in enterprise software (Microsoft 365, Azure, Dynamics)
- Network effects through Teams, Office ecosystem
- 75% desktop OS market share
- 400+ datacenters across 70 regions globally (more than any other cloud provider)
- Strategic ownership stake in OpenAI - generative AI leadership
- Economies of scale and globally recognized brand
- Unified platform (Azure + M365 + Dynamics) creates deep lock-in

## Key Risks
- Significant capex increase: $34.9 billion in Q1 FY2026, with guidance that FY2026 capex growth rate will exceed FY2025 rate
- Competition from AWS (Amazon) and Google Cloud in cloud market
- AI investment ROI uncertainty - market concern over whether AI spend will translate to revenue
- Regulatory scrutiny (antitrust, EU)
