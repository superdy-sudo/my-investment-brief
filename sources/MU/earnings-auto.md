## Quarter: Q1 FY2026 (Micron fiscal year ends August)

## Beat / Miss
- STRONG BEAT on both EPS and Revenue
- EPS: $4.78 vs estimate $3.94 (beat by 21.3%)
- Revenue: $13.64B vs estimate $12.88B (beat by 5.9%)
- YoY Revenue growth: +57%
- Sequential Revenue growth: +21%

## Revenue Breakdown
- DRAM: $10.8 billion (79% of total revenue)
- NAND: $2.7 billion (21% of total revenue)
- Cloud Memory Business Unit: nearly doubled to $5.28B with 66% gross margin

## Margins
- Record-high Gross Margin: 56.8% (vs ~40% full year FY2025)
- Driven by: higher pricing, cost efficiency, favorable product mix (HBM)

## Guidance (Q2 FY2026)
- Non-GAAP Revenue: $18.7B +/- $400M (massive +37% sequential jump guided)
- Gross Margin guidance: ~68% +/- 100 bps (further expansion expected)

## Management Tone
- Very bullish - "record quarter"
- AI memory demand acceleration called out explicitly
- HBM supply sold out, customers in long-term agreements
- Stock reaction: +9% on earnings day
