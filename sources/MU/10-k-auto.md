# Micron Technology (MU) - 10-K Annual Report Key Highlights
Source: SEC EDGAR Filing FY2025
URL: https://www.stocktitan.net/sec-filings/MU/10-k-micron-technology-inc-files-annual-report-a879767daea7.html
Direct SEC: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0000723125/000072312525000028/mu-20250828.htm

## Revenue Trend
- FY2025 Revenue: +49% YoY
- DRAM Revenue: $28.58 billion (vs $17.60B in FY2024, vs $10.98B in FY2023)
- DRAM growth: +62% YoY (driven by higher ASPs and bit shipments)
- NAND growth: +18% YoY
- Net Income: $8,539 million in FY2025
- Total Equity: $54,165 million as of Aug 28, 2025

## Margins
- Gross Margin FY2025: 40% (vs 22% in FY2024)
- Improvement driven by: price recovery, manufacturing cost improvements, favorable inventory mix

## Moat / Competitive Advantages
- One of only three major DRAM manufacturers globally (oligopoly: Samsung, SK Hynix, Micron)
- HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) leadership for AI applications
  - HBM revenue crossed $1 billion in Q2 FY2025
  - Q3 FY2025: nearly 50% sequential HBM revenue growth
  - FY2025 HBM supply sold out; customers signed to long-term agreements into 2026
- HBM carries structurally higher margins than commodity DRAM
- CHIPS Act grants: $6.4 billion approved
- Advanced packaging capabilities expansion in U.S.
- Brands: Micron and Crucial

## Key Risks
- Highly cyclical industry - memory prices historically revert
- Geopolitical risk (China exposure, export controls)
- Competition from Samsung and SK Hynix in HBM
- Capital intensive - requires constant heavy investment in manufacturing
- NAND market remains more competitive and commoditized
