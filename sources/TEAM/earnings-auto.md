### Segment Highlights
- **Cloud revenue:** $1,132M (+29% YoY)

- **Remaining Performance Obligations (RPO):** $3,996M (+37% YoY) — indicator of future contracted revenue
- **Enterprise RPO:** $4.0B (+37% YoY) — customers signing bigger, longer-term contracts

### AI Metrics
- AI credit usage: growing >20% month-over-month
- Rovo (AI platform): customers using Rovo are growing ARR at ~2x rate vs non-Rovo customers
- Teamwork Collection (AI bundled): described as "significant growth engine" driving ARPU expansion

### FCF
- Q3 FY2026 FCF: $561.3M (31% FCF margin) — strong rebound vs Q1 ($115M) and Q2 ($168.5M)

### Management Commentary
CEO Mike Cannon-Brookes: "Our strong Q3 results show the power of our strategy in action, with total revenue growing 32% year-over-year to $1.8 billion, as customers sign bigger, longer-term commitments, and connect their teams and workflows on our AI-powered platform."

Key themes:
1. Enterprise expansion: larger contracts, longer durations — RPO growth (+37%) leading revenue growth (+32%)
2. AI monetization: Rovo driving measurable ARR acceleration for adopters; AI credits = new upsell vector
3. Teamwork Collection bundle: packaging Jira + Confluence + Loom + Rovo into integrated suite → ARPU expansion
4. Data Center to Cloud migration: still significant runway; DC customers at higher ARPU when they migrate to Cloud with AI features
