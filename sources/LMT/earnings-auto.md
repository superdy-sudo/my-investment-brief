## Quarter: Q1 2026 (reported April 23, 2026)

## Beat / Miss
- MISS on both EPS and Revenue
- EPS: $6.44 vs estimate $6.77 (missed by ~4.9%)
- Revenue: $18.0B vs estimate $18.4B (missed by ~2.2%)
- YoY Revenue: essentially flat
- YoY Net Earnings: $1.5B vs $1.7B prior year (down ~12%)

## Cash Flow - Critical Issue
- Cash from Operations Q1 2026: $220 million (vs $1.4B in Q1 2025) - down 84%
- Free Cash Flow Q1 2026: -$291 million (vs +$955 million in Q1 2025)
- Full year 2026 FCF target: $6 billion (management reaffirmed)

## Guidance
- Full year 2026 sales guidance: $77.5B - $80.0B (reaffirmed)
- Guidance held despite weak Q1

## Management Tone
- Defensive - reaffirmed annual guidance but did not raise
- Acknowledged margin compression from classified program issues
- Highlighted record $194B backlog as long-term strength

## Key Concerns
- Margin compression is real and ongoing
- FCF collapse in Q1 is a significant red flag for cash-generative thesis
- Stock reaction: -6.3% on earnings day
