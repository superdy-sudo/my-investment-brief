## Key Business Highlights

**AI Semiconductor (Growth Engine):**
- Q1 AI revenue $8.4B — above internal forecast
- Driven by: Custom AI Accelerators (XPUs) สำหรับ hyperscalers + AI networking (Ethernet switches)
- Q2 guidance $10.7B implies continued acceleration

**VMware/Infrastructure Software:**
- ยังคงเป็น stable, high-margin revenue stream
- VMware Cloud Foundation subscription adoption เพิ่มขึ้น

**FY2025 Full Year (context):**
- Revenue: $63.9B
- Adjusted EBITDA: $43.0B (+35% YoY) — record
- Free Cash Flow: $26.9B
