## Item 1 — Business Description

IDEXX Laboratories is a global leader in veterinary diagnostics, software, and water/livestock testing. The company serves companion animal veterinarians, livestock producers, and water quality testing labs worldwide.

**Business Segments:**

1. **Companion Animal Group (CAG)** — ~90%+ of revenue
   - Point-of-care diagnostic instruments (Catalyst One, ProCyte One, SediVue)
   - In-clinic analyzers + consumable reagents (annuity-like recurring revenue)
   - IDEXX Reference Laboratories (outsourced lab testing)
   - Veterinary software (Cornerstone, Neo, ezyVet) and cloud services
   - Imaging systems and digital radiography

2. **Water** — ~5% of revenue
   - Colilert, Quanti-Tray, and other water quality testing products
   - Sold to municipal/utility customers globally

3. **Livestock, Poultry and Dairy (LPD)** — ~5% of revenue
   - Diagnostic tests for food animal health and food safety

**Key competitive advantages (Moat):**
- High switching costs: once a veterinary practice installs IDEXX analyzers, reagents are proprietary to those machines
- Installed base of instruments creates annuity-like consumables revenue stream
- Reference lab network spans 40+ countries with deep logistics
- Software ecosystem (practice management) creates additional lock-in
- Strong brand trust among veterinary professionals globally
