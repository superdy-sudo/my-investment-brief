## Item 1 - Business

Palantir develops data integration and AI software for government and commercial sectors.

**Four main platforms:**
1. **Gotham** - Government/defense operations intelligence platform
2. **Foundry** - Enterprise data operating system
3. **Apollo** - Cloud-agnostic continuous delivery platform
4. **AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)** - Generative AI with LLM integration

The "Palantir Ontology" is the foundational technology, mapping data systematically to organizational context.

## Item 1A - Risk Factors (Top Risks)

1. **Revenue Sustainability** - "We may not be able to sustain our revenue growth in the future"
2. **Sales Cycle Challenges** - Long, unpredictable cycles (6-9 months, sometimes longer)
3. **Customer Concentration** - Top 3 customers represent 16% of revenue
4. **Contract Uncertainty** - Clients can terminate for convenience
5. **AI-related Risks** - Reputational harm and liability from AI deployment
6. **Government Dependency** - Budget delays and policy changes threaten revenue
