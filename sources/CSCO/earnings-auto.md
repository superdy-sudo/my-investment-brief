### Management Commentary
CEO Chuck Robbins: "Cisco delivered record quarterly revenue in Q3 and we saw very strong, broad-based demand for our products, demonstrating the relevance of our technology for connecting and securing AI."

Key themes from management:
1. AI infrastructure is driving demand well beyond prior expectations — hyperscaler orders the key catalyst
2. Campus networking renaissance: enterprises upgrading for AI-enabled workplaces
3. Splunk integration: full-stack observability and security bundling starting to resonate with enterprise buyers
4. Restructuring ($1B pretax, $450M in Q4 FY2026): refocusing on silicon, optics, security, AI — eliminating redundant roles from Splunk integration
