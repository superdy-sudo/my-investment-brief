## Q1 FY2026 Results
**Source:** https://www.stocktitan.net/news/SNPS/synopsys-posts-financial-results-for-first-quarter-fiscal-year-ika7xvp784c5.html
**SEC Filing (8-K):** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0000883241/000119312526071601/d921168dex991.htm

- **Revenue:** $2.409B (vs $1.455B Q1 FY2025, +65.6% YoY — includes Ansys contribution)
- **Non-GAAP EPS:** $3.77 (beat estimate of $3.11, exceeded by ~21%)
- **Ansys Revenue Contribution:** $886M (37% of total revenue)
- **Operating Cash Flow:** $856.83M (swung from negative territory a year ago)
- **Long-term Debt Reduction:** ~$3.45B repaid during the quarter
