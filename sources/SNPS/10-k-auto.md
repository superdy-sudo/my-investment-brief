## Item 1 - Business

Synopsys positions itself as "the leader in engineering solutions from silicon to systems."

**Two main segments:**
1. **Design Automation** - Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools, now includes Ansys simulation & analysis (S&A) software
2. **Design IP** - Silicon IP portfolio

**Key 2025 development:** Acquisition of ANSYS, Inc. completed, adding multiphysics S&A tools across aerospace, automotive, energy, and other industries. Headcount increased ~40% to approximately 28,000 employees globally (189 offices worldwide).

**AI growth driver:** Synopsys.ai stack spanning design, verification, and manufacturing workflows.

## Item 1A - Risk Factors (Top Risks)

1. **Ansys Integration Risk** - Synergy realization and margin impacts from merger integration remain uncertain
2. **Macroeconomic Uncertainty** - Global conditions could affect customer license renewals
3. **Trade Regulations** - Export controls and tariffs, particularly affecting China operations
4. **Competition** - Rival EDA vendors and customers building internal tools
5. **Talent Retention** - Attracting/retaining senior management and key engineering talent
