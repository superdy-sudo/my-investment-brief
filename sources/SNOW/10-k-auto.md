## Item 1 - Business

Snowflake's mission is to help every enterprise achieve its potential with data and AI. The platform is designed to be:
- Easy to use
- Connected (data flows seamlessly across teams and organizations)
- Trusted (governance and security enabling confident operations)

The company provides support for modern data architectures through:
- Data engineering
- Data governance
- Collaboration and analytics
- AI innovation across the end-to-end data lifecycle

**Key customers:** Exxon Mobil, Honeywell, London Stock Exchange Group among thousands of enterprise clients.

**Product Revenue FY2025:** $3.5 billion, representing 30% YoY growth.

---

## Item 1A - Risk Factors (Top Risks)

- Growth dependent on continued adoption of cloud and AI-native data platforms
- Highly competitive market (AWS, Azure, Google, Databricks)
- Net revenue retention rate could decline if customers reduce usage
- Operating losses: company has historically operated at a loss
- Stock-based compensation is significant and dilutive
